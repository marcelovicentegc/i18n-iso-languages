# Changelog

## [0.6.0] - 11/21/2020

### Added

- Language support for:

  - English (G.B.)
  - Chinese (Standard)
  - Hindi
  - Spanish (Mexico, Colombia and Spain)
  - French
  - Arabic (Egypt, Standard and U.A.E.)
  - Bengali
  - Indonesian
  - Urdu
  - German (Germany and Austria)
  - Catalan

- ISO 639-2 field on `Locale` interface.

### Fixed

- English (U.S.A.) and Portuguese (Brazil) ISO 639-1
